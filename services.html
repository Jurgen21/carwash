U TURN 4 CAR WASH SERVICES

Experience the ultimate car care with our comprehensive washing and detailing services. From exterior shine to interior perfection, we keep your vehicle looking its best.

Surface Wipe

We’ll carefully clean and restore the surfaces you interact with most in your vehicle. Using premium microfibre cloths, detailing brushes, and specialist cleaning products, we’ll wipe away grime, fingerprints, and smudges from areas like the dashboard, center console, door handles, and steering wheel. This process leaves a fresh and polished look, enhancing the cleanliness and hygiene of your car’s interior.

Glass Cleaning

Crystal-clear visibility is essential for safe driving, and our glass cleaning service ensures just that. We use professional-grade glass cleaners to remove smudges, streaks, and dirt from your vehicle’s windows, both inside and out. The result is spotless, streak-free glass that improves visibility and adds to the aesthetic appeal of your car.

Tyre Dressing

The blacker the wheel, the sweeter they look. A liberal coat of Autoglym® Tyre Dressing applied to your tyres will have them looking like new, and provide the strongest possible visual contrast to your pristine shining alloys.

Hand Polish

Better be careful you don’t dazzle anyone else on the roads, after we’ve polished your vehicle’s bodywork using Autoglym® Radiant Polish and hand buffed it to an ethereal shine with a mirror-like finish.

Deep Clean Mats

You wouldn’t believe the colour of the water that comes out of a floor mat that “looks” clean. After vacuuming all the loose dirt and debris out of them, we’ll wet-dry clean the mat fibres to restore them to a brand new, light and fluffy finish.

Leather Care

Proper leather care is crucial to preserving its appearance and longevity, preventing cracks and tears. We’ll clean visible marks and stains, before treating all the interior leather surfaces with Autoglym® Leather Care Cream.

Plastic Condition

Your vehicle’s plastic trim is a high-contact area and easily subject to dirt and damage. A careful treatment with Autoglym® Trim & Plastic Conditioner will restore and protect your vehicle’s trim, leaving a clean, flawless finish.

HD Wax

Autoglym® HD Wax creates the ultimate exterior body shine with an immersive, deep-gloss finish that emphasises and protects your paintwork. HD Wax goes beyond showroom shine with a beautiful, lasting, water-repellent finish.

Interior Shampoo

You wouldn’t neglect the carpets and furniture in your home, so why should your car be any different? We use Autoglym® interior cleaning products to remove marks, spills, and stains, restoring and protecting your fabric surfaces.

Engine Bay Clean

Your car will thank you for the occasional engine clean. It’s a hotspot for trapped dirt and debris, as well as the dust and grime your engine routinely produces. A true specialist detail isn’t complete without a shiny, clean engine bay.

Paint Protection

Our paint protection service applies a high-quality sealant to your car’s paintwork, shielding it from contaminants, bird droppings, and environmental damage. This treatment not only preserves the paint but also enhances the shine, keeping your vehicle looking pristine for longer.

Headlight Restoration

Over time, headlights can become cloudy and discolored. Our headlight restoration service cleans and polishes the lenses, removing oxidation and restoring them to a like-new condition, improving both the appearance and functionality of your headlights.

Rim Cleaning

Our rim cleaning service removes brake dust, dirt, and grime from your vehicle’s rims, restoring their original shine. We use specialized cleaners and tools to ensure your rims are spotless and gleaming, improving the overall appearance of your car.

Deep Interior Clean

Our deep interior clean service goes beyond a basic vacuuming, thoroughly cleaning every surface inside your vehicle. We remove dust, dirt, and debris from hard-to-reach places, ensuring a fresh and spotless interior from top to bottom.

Odor Elimination

Our odor elimination service removes lingering smells from your car’s interior. Whether it’s from pets, food, or smoke, we use specialized treatments to neutralize unpleasant odors, leaving your car smelling fresh and clean.
